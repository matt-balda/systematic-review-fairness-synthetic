## Web of Science (WoS) Search String
## Filters: 2021–2026, English, Article / Proceedings Paper

TS=(
  (
    bias* OR "algorithmic bias" OR "bias mitigation"
    OR fair* OR unfair* OR "algorithmic fairness"
    OR equity OR inequity
  )
  AND
  (
    "synthetic data" OR "synthetic dataset"
    OR "synthetic data generation"
    OR "simulated data"
    OR "data synthesis"
  )
  AND
  (
    "artificial intelligence" OR "deep learning" OR "neural network*"
    OR algorithm*
  )
  AND
  (
    clinical OR healthcare
    OR "medical data" OR "health data"
    OR "patient data"
  )
)

## Filters to apply in the interface:
## - Publication Years: 2021–2026
## - Language: English
## - Document Types: Article, Proceedings Paper
## - Exclude: Review Article, Editorial Material, Letter, Book Chapter
##
## TS = Topic (searches Title, Abstract, Author Keywords, and Keywords Plus)
