## Inclusion criteria
- Studies that use deep learning methods for the generation of synthetic medical data.
- Studies that address the application of these technologies with the aim of promoting fairness or mitigating bias, particularly in underrepresented groups.
- Studies published between 2020 and 2026.
- Peer-reviewed publications, including journal articles and conference papers.

## Exclusion criteria
- Studies not published in English or without full-text availability.
- Studies not focused on human healthcare.
- Studies that do not involve synthetic data or do not address fairness and/or bias.
- Grey literature, editorials, opinion papers, and non-peer-reviewed works.
- Literature reviews, commentaries, meta-analyses, books, clinical trials, and randomized controlled trials.
